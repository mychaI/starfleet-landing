Instantly generates GraphQL services from your MongoDB/mongoose schemas, with the ability to deploy them to a Docker container.

Learn More

Simplify Your GraphQL Server Production

Starfleet is a command line tool that lets you easily harness the flexibility of MongoDB and combine it with the declarative power of GraphQL.

Like our open source project? Star us on Github!

Star

Init

Get GraphQL services by providing your MongoDB/Mongoose Schemas' path, connection URI, and name of your database.

Deploy

Deploy your project with GraphQL services that were generated by Starfleet to the Docker containers.
